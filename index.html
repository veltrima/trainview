The Studio

Little space, big sound.

Located in a bachelor appartment in Midtown Toronto, we make great use of the small space.


Effects/Plugins: Many synth VSTs, full EastWest orchestral suite, guitar amp plugin, virtual drums, an assortment of effects (reverb/flanger/delay/etc.), iZotope Ozone mastering plugin


In addition to a full suite of effects and plugins, we have: dynamic and condenser mics, a full 88-key MIDI keyboard, an electric drum kit, an APC40 controller, miniature "sound booth"


We have an assortment of instruments on hand as well: Acoustic and electric guitars, electric bass, banjo, mandolin, ukelele, dizi, kalimba, harmonica, and even a penny whistle!
